Extra Credit Assignments

Extra Credit

Each student is entitled to ONE extra credit assignment per quarter. Choose wisely!

  • Create a Hero - Write a full 3 page essay detailing a hero of your own creation.
  • Vocabulary Flashcards - Create flashcards on our latest vocab list (30 words).
  • Villain Essay - Research a villain and write a 3 page essay on his/her best crimes.
  • Power Poster - Create an artistic poster showing common and uncommon hero catchphrases.
